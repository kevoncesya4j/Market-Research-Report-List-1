<p><ul><li>UV Disinfection Robot</li><li>HPV Disinfection Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air disinfection robot market features two main types: UV disinfection robots and HPV disinfection robots. UV disinfection robots utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate airborne pathogens, effectively reducing the spread of infectious diseases in various environments like hospitals and public spaces. In contrast, HPV (hydrogen peroxide vapor) disinfection robots disperse vaporized hydrogen peroxide to sanitize air and surfaces, providing deep disinfection that can reach inaccessible areas. Both technologies aim to enhance air quality and safety in settings requiring rigorous hygiene standards.</p></p>
