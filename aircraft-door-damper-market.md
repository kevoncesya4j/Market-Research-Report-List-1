<p><p>The Aircraft Door Damper market is categorized into three main types: Rotary Aircraft Dampers, Linear Aircraft Dampers, and Axial Aircraft Dampers. Rotary Aircraft Dampers utilize rotational motion to control door movement, providing smooth operation. Linear Aircraft Dampers function by absorbing shocks through a straight-line motion mechanism, enhancing stability. Axial Aircraft Dampers are designed for applications that involve axial forces, ensuring effective dampening during door operation. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing safety and operational efficiency in aviation.</p></p>
