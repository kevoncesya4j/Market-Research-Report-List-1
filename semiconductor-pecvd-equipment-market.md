<p><p>The semiconductor PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment market is segmented into Anode PECVD and Cathode PECVD systems. Anode PECVD systems utilize an anode electrode where the substrate is placed to generate thin films through plasma interactions, commonly used for insulating layers. In contrast, Cathode PECVD systems employ a cathode electrode, enhancing film deposition rates and plasma density, ideal for applications requiring high-quality coatings. Both types serve crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting device performance and production efficiency.</p></p>
