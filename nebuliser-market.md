<p><strong>The Nebuliser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebuliser</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebuliser</li><li>Mesh Nebuliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nebuliser market includes three main types: pneumatic nebulisers, ultrasonic nebulisers, and mesh nebulisers. Pneumatic nebulisers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medicine into aerosol droplets, making them suitable for a wide range of medications. Ultrasonic nebulisers use high-frequency sound waves to produce mist, offering quieter operation and faster drug delivery. Mesh nebulisers employ a vibrating mesh to create aerosol, ensuring a fine mist with minimal residual medication, enhancing efficiency and patient compliance in respiratory therapies.</p></p>
