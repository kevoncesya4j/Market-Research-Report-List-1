<p><p>The pharmaceutical 3D printing market includes various methods for creating drug delivery systems and personalized pharmaceuticals. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) involves extruding thermoplastic materials to shape objects. Melt Extrusion Deposition (MED) uses a similar principle but focuses on molten materials for drug formulation. Powder Binding (PB) adheres powder particles to form solid structures, while Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) utilizes lasers to fuse materials. Stereolithography (SLA) employs light to cure liquid resins into solid forms. Other methods may include binder jetting and digital light processing (DLP).</p></p>
