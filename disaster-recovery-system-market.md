<p><p>Disaster Recovery Systems (DRS) are categorized into on-premises and cloud-based markets. On-premises systems involve local infrastructure, where organizations manage their own hardware and software to protect data and applications from disasters. This provides greater control but requires substantial investment. In contrast, cloud-based systems leverage off-site cloud services, allowing businesses to access recovery resources and scalability without maintaining physical infrastructure. This option typically offers lower upfront costs and faster recovery times, making it suitable for many organizations seeking flexibility.</p></p>
