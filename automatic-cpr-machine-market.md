<p><p>The Automatic CPR Machine market features two primary types: pneumatically driven and electrically powered devices. In contrast, electrically powered machines rely on electric motors, enabling precise control over compression rates and depths. Both types aim to improve the efficacy of cardiopulmonary resuscitation in critical care settings, enhancing patient outcomes during cardiac arrest by providing uninterrupted and consistent support.</p></p>
