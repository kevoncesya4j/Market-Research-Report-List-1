<p><p>The flue gas measuring instruments market is divided into two main types: continuous flue gas measuring and non-continuous flue gas measuring. Continuous flue gas measuring instruments provide real-time monitoring of emissions, allowing for immediate detection of changes in gas composition, essential for regulatory compliance and operational efficiency. In contrast, non-continuous flue gas measuring involves periodic sampling and analysis, which can be less expensive but may not capture transient emission variations. Both types play vital roles in environmental management and industrial processes.</p></p>
