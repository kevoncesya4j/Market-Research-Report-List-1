<p><strong>The Mercury Emissions Analyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mercury emissions analyzer market includes two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF) mercury analyzers. CVAA utilizes cold vapor technology to measure mercury concentrations by absorbing specific wavelengths of light after vaporizing samples, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, CAF employs fluorescence principles, where mercury vapor emits light when excited, providing rapid and highly sensitive measurements. Both technologies are crucial for environmental monitoring and compliance in industries concerned with mercury emissions.</p></p>
