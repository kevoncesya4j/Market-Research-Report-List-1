<p><strong>The Thermopile Laser Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volume Absorber Sensors</li><li>Surface Absorber Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermopile Laser Sensor market consists of two primary types: Volume Absorber Sensors and Surface Absorber Sensors. Volume Absorber Sensors capture temperature changes by sensing the absorption of laser energy within a bulk medium, making them ideal for detecting gas concentrations. In contrast, Surface Absorber Sensors focus on detecting temperature changes on surfaces or thin films, allowing for precise measurements in various applications, including industrial and environmental monitoring. Both types leverage thermopile technology for accurate detection and measurement.</p></p>
