<p><strong>The Heart-Lung Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump</li><li>Double Roller Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heart-Lung Machines market consists of two main types: Single Roller Pumps and Double Roller Pumps. Single Roller Pumps utilize a single roller to move blood through the circuit, offering simplicity and reliability, which makes them suitable for various surgical procedures. In contrast, Double Roller Pumps feature two rollers for enhanced blood flow management and reduced shear stress on blood cells, making them ideal for complex surgeries. Both types are essential for ensuring effective cardiopulmonary bypass during heart surgeries.</p></p>
