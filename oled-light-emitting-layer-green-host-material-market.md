<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Material</li><li>Phosphorescent Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED light-emitting layer green host material market comprises two main types: fluorescent and phosphorescent materials. Fluorescent materials emit light through the rapid re-emission of absorbed energy, typically resulting in lower efficiency and brightness. In contrast, phosphorescent materials, which utilize triplet excitons to produce light, offer higher efficiency and longer emission durations, enhancing brightness and color fidelity. The choice between these materials significantly impacts the performance, lifespan, and application of OLED displays in various electronic devices.</p></p>
