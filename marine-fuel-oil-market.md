<p><strong>The Marine Fuel Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distillate Fuel Oil</li><li>Residual Fuel Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine fuel oil market primarily consists of two types: distillate fuel oil and residual fuel oil. Distillate fuel oil, which includes marine gas oil (MGO), is refined to meet stricter emissions regulations and is suitable for cleaner-burning engines. In contrast, residual fuel oil, often referred to as heavy fuel oil, is less expensive but contains higher levels of contaminants and sulfur. Its use is declining due to environmental regulations, pushing the market towards cleaner alternatives.</p></p>
