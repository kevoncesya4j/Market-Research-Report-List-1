<p><strong>The Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Anemometer</li><li>Pressure Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anemometer market comprises two main types: velocity anemometers and pressure anemometers. Velocity anemometers measure the speed of airflow, utilizing various technologies such as thermodynamic, hot-wire, or ultrasonic methods to provide accurate wind velocity readings, essential for HVAC, meteorology, and environmental monitoring. Pressure anemometers, on the other hand, assess airflow based on pressure differentials, primarily used in industrial applications for ventilation and air quality control. Together, these devices play crucial roles in various sectors where airflow measurement is critical.</p></p>
