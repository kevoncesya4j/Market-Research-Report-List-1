<p><p>The forklift solid tire market comprises two main types: cured-on solid tires and pressed-on solid tires. Cured-on solid tires are manufactured through a curing process that enhances durability and performance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, pressed-on solid tires are created by compressing rubber compounds onto a metal core, offering a lightweight and cost-effective solution. Both types provide excellent traction and puncture resistance, catering to different operational needs in industrial and warehouse environments.</p></p>
