<p><ul><li>Synthetic Material</li><li>Natural Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioabsorbable surgical implant market is categorized into two main types based on material composition: synthetic and natural materials. Synthetic materials, such as polylactic acid and polyglycolic acid, are engineered for specific mechanical and degradation properties, providing controlled absorption rates. In contrast, natural materials, derived from biological sources like collagen or chitosan, offer biocompatibility and often promote tissue healing. Both types are increasingly utilized in surgical applications due to their ability to reduce the need for follow-up surgeries to remove implants.</p></p>
