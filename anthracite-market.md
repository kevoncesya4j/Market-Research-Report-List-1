<p><p>The anthracite market is primarily divided into two types: lump anthracite and anthracite fines. Lump anthracite consists of larger, easily handled pieces, typically used for industrial applications and heating due to its high carbon content and low impurities. In contrast, anthracite fines are smaller particles produced during mining and processing, often utilized in steel production and as a filtration medium. Both types serve distinct purposes within energy and industrial sectors, affecting supply chains and pricing dynamics.</p></p>
