<p><p>The silica gel market is categorized into three main types based on their adsorption properties and applications. Type A silica gel features a uniform pore structure, ideal for moisture control and drying agents. Type B silica gel has a wider pore size distribution, enhancing its ability to adsorb larger molecules, often used in various industrial applications. Type C silica gel is specially formulated with a high surface area for selective adsorption, commonly utilized in specialized applications like gas purification and chromatography.</p></p>
