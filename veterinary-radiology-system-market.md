<p><p>The veterinary radiology system market comprises two primary types: digital X-ray systems and analog X-ray systems. Digital X-ray systems utilize advanced technology to capture images electronically, allowing for instant viewing, enhanced image quality, and easier storage and sharing. In contrast, analog X-ray systems rely on traditional film methods, which involve longer processing times and physical film handling. Both systems serve vital roles in diagnosing animal health issues, but digital systems are increasingly favored for their efficiency and effectiveness in veterinary practices.</p></p>
