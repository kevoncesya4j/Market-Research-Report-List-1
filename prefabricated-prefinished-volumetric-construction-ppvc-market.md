<p><p>Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC) involves constructing building modules off-site that are pre-finished, then transported and assembled on-site. The PPVC market is categorized primarily into concrete and fiber-reinforced modules. Concrete PPVC offers strength and durability, making it suitable for various building types. In contrast, fiber-reinforced PPVC utilizes composite materials for added flexibility and reduced weight, facilitating easier transport and installation. Both types enhance construction efficiency, reduce waste, and minimize on-site labor requirements, promoting sustainable building practices.</p></p>
