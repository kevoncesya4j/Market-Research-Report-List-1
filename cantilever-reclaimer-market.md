<p><p>The cantilever reclaimer market comprises two primary types: wheel and chain reclaimers. Wheel reclaimers utilize rotating wheels equipped with buckets to scoop and transport bulk materials, offering high maneuverability and efficiency in smaller or uneven terrains. Conversely, chain reclaimers employ a continuous chain system with attached buckets, allowing for robust material handling over larger areas and heavier loads. Both types are essential for efficiently managing and reclaiming bulk materials in industries such as mining, ports, and construction.</p></p>
