<p><ul><li>Earth Pressure Balance Machines</li><li>Slurry Shield (SS)</li><li>Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT)</li><li>Auger Boring</li><li>Vacuum Extraction TBM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro tunnel boring machine (MTBM) market consists of various types designed for specific tunneling conditions. Earth Pressure Balance Machines (EPB) manage soil pressure during excavation. Slurry Shield machines use a mixture of water and soil for stability. Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT) utilizes pilot tubes for precise alignment. Auger Boring employs a rotating helical screw for soil removal, while Vacuum Extraction TBMs rely on suction methods. Other variants cater to unique geological conditions and project requirements, enhancing versatility and efficiency in tunneling.</p></p>
