<p><p>Fatty Acid Methyl Ester (FAME) is a bio-derived product made by transesterifying lipids, such as vegetable oils or animal fats, with methanol. The market for FAME is driven by the increasing demand for sustainable energy sources and stringent environmental regulations promoting the use of renewable fuels. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a growing focus on the development of advanced biofuels, including second and third-generation FAME produced from non-food feedstocks.
