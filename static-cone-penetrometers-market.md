<p><ul><li>Hand Operated Static Cone Penetrometer</li><li>Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The static cone penetrometer market is categorized into two primary types: hand-operated static cone penetrometers and self-start static cone penetrometers. Hand-operated models require manual effort to drive the cone into the ground, making them suitable for smaller or more localized testing situations. In contrast, self-start static cone penetrometers utilize mechanical systems to automate penetration, improving efficiency and accuracy. Both types are essential for assessing soil properties and support various construction, engineering, and geotechnical applications.</p></p>
