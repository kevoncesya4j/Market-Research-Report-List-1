<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aviation Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNR headsets</li><li>ANR headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation headsets market is primarily categorized into Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) and Active Noise Reduction (ANR) headsets. PNR headsets use physical design and materials to block ambient noise, providing a basic level of sound isolation. In contrast, ANR headsets incorporate electronics to actively cancel unwanted sounds, offering superior noise reduction and enhancing audio clarity. ANR headsets are increasingly favored by pilots for long flights, while PNR models remain popular for budget-conscious users.</p></p>
