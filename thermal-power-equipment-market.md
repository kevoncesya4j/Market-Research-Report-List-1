<p><p>The thermal power equipment market encompasses essential components such as boilers, steam turbines, and generators. Boilers generate steam from water, using fuel combustion, to create the high-pressure steam necessary for power generation. Steam turbines convert this high-pressure steam into mechanical energy, driving the rotor to produce electricity. Generators then transform this mechanical energy into electrical energy, enabling power distribution. Together, these elements form the backbone of thermal power plants, providing reliable energy generation to meet growing demand.</p></p>
