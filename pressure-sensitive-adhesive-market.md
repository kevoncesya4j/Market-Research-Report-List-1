<p><p>The pressure-sensitive adhesive market comprises three main types: acrylic, rubber, and silicone. Acrylic adhesives offer excellent weather resistance, durability, and UV stability, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Rubber adhesives provide strong initial tack and flexibility, commonly used in packaging and automotive industries. Silicone adhesives feature high-temperature resistance and exceptional performance on challenging substrates, suitable for electronics and medical applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on properties like adhesion strength, environmental resistance, and temperature tolerance, catering to various industries.</p></p>
