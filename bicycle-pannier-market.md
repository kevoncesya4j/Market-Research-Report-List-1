<p><p>Bicycle panniers are divided into two main market types: waterproof and non-waterproof. Waterproof panniers are designed to keep contents dry and secure, making them ideal for cyclists who ride in various weather conditions or take longer trips. They often feature sealed seams and water-resistant materials. Non-waterproof panniers, while generally lighter and more affordable, may not offer the same level of protection from rain. They suit recreational cyclists or those who primarily ride in dry conditions, focusing on storage convenience over weather resistance.</p></p>
